Our Mission

52 Weeks Development exists to empower returning citizens by providing hands-on job training, apprenticeship opportunities, trauma-informed counseling, and career placement—helping them transition from incarceration to hope, stability, and opportunity.

Our Story

Founded in 2018 to support at-risk youth, we evolved to address the critical needs of returning citizens facing barriers to reentry.

The Model

Combining YouthBuild and Job Corps frameworks to provide holistic education, workforce, and therapeutic support.

Who We Serve

Justice-involved individuals aged 18-54 in Broward and Dade Counties seeking a structured path forward.

Workforce Development & Job Training

Equip yourself with essential skills for high-demand careers through hands-on training and apprenticeships with local businesses for real-world experience.

  • ✓
    Industry-Specific Training: Construction, healthcare, technology, hospitality.
  • ✓
    NCCER Core Training: Safety, construction basics, and workplace skills.
  • ✓
    Trade Pathways: Masonry, plumbing, carpentry, and other trades.
  • ✓
    Apprenticeship Opportunities: Paid work experience pipelines.
  • ✓
    Job Placement Assistance: Resume building and employer connections.

Mental Health Support

Comprehensive trauma-informed counseling and wellness services to support emotional wellbeing and personal growth.

  • ✓
    Individual Counseling: One-on-one sessions with licensed therapists.
  • ✓
    Group Therapy: Peer support groups fostering connection.
  • ✓
    Trauma-Informed Care: Addressing impact of past trauma.
  • ✓
    Wellness Programs: Mindfulness and stress management.

FAQ

How can I apply for your programs?
Please contact us directly using the form above or call our office to begin the application process. We will guide you through the necessary steps.
Do you offer services for family members of justice-involved individuals?
Yes, we believe in a holistic approach and can provide resources and referrals for family members.
How can businesses partner with 52 Weeks Development?
We are always looking for partners for apprenticeships and job placement. Please reach out via email to discuss partnership opportunities.
Is my donation tax-deductible?
Yes, 52 Weeks Development is a registered non-profit organization, and your donations are tax-deductible.
